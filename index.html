Chengyi Xing

I am a second year Master's student in CCRMA at Stanford University. I am currently doing research in Biomimetics & Dexterous Manipulation Laboratory, advised by Mark Cutkosky.

I am interested in tactile sensors, especially wearable tactile sensors and tactile sensor for underwater robot Ocean1K. Additionally, I am also interested in dexterous hand manipulation. .

Besides robotics, I am also exploring interdisplinay research in Music, Healthcare, Hardware, and AI. Currently, I am fortunate to have potential collaboration with Professor Elizabeth Schumann, Mac Schwager and Michael Ma.
